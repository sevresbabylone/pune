Life in a Metro

Growing up online and offline in an Indian city

In a country where the internet carries the hopes, dreams and desires of a whole new generation, this website explores what it means to come of age as a young person in India, both in real life and on the internet.

Based on seven months of ethnographic fieldwork studying the everyday lives of lower-middle class youth in Pune, this website captures a day in the life of a typical college student in a north Indian city.
